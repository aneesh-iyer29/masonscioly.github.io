SCHOOL MAP + PARKING (2022 onwards)

Buses can drop off at the main doors in the front but should park in the bus circle at the back of the building past the community center and athletic wing. Cars can park by the athletic center and enter through the music door, then walk straight down the hallway until reaching the large commons.

A block schedule with finalized room numbers will be emailed out as we near the invite date. Please keep in mind that we will only be using C and D Pod of the school for this invite and since we have 12 identical pods, it is important to ensure that you always know where you are in relation to the main help areas.
